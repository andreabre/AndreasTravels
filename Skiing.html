I love to ski. It is probably my favorite sport. I think it geniuenly really helped me out during a time I was not doing the best in, so I am very grateful for it. My favorite ski resorts are Park City in Utah, Mammoth Mountain in California, and Snow Basin in Utah.

Skiing is such an amazing and fun sport! Though difficult to pick up, once you know it, it is a blast. It is best to learn at the start of Winter! Here it is not too cold yet, but the snow is nice and fluffy, and not slushy!
